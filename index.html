how secure will my info be?

the info you input here will be sent to the maelink server, where it will be hashed and therefore irreversible. the only ways your unhashed data can be stolen are you voluntarily sharing it, and an attacker intercepting/spying on your requests or screen.

should I refrain from entering my info?

despite the text above, no. your username is only stored in the maelink database, your password strength is checked locally using zxcvbn, and everything you input in this form or any other form on this website is sent to the maelink server only.

is this client good?

hopefully! you can make it better by sending feedback to the developer, but ideally this client should be simple enough to navigate and be packed with as many features as possible. for now, it's still in development so not yet on that one sadly.
